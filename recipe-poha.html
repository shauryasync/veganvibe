Quick & Easy Masala Poha

Prep: 5 mins • Cook: 10 mins • Serves: 2

A light, flavorful, and quick breakfast or snack made with flattened rice, spices, and a touch of lemon.

Back to Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups medium-thick Poha (flattened rice)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • 1/4 cup peanuts
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt and sugar to taste
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon, and fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Gently rinse the poha in a colander under running water for about 30 seconds. Drain completely and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds. Once they splutter, add asafoetida, green chili, and peanuts. Fry until peanuts are crunchy.
  3. Add chopped onion and sauté until translucent. Stir in turmeric powder and salt.
  4. Add the rinsed poha to the pan. Mix gently until the turmeric coats all the poha evenly. Cover and steam for 2 minutes.
  5. Turn off the heat. Sprinkle with sugar (optional) and lemon juice. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.
