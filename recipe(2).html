Vegan Sausage Pasta

Prep: 10 mins • Cook: 25 mins • Serves: 4

A comforting, rich, and flavorful pasta dish using plant-based Italian sausages and a creamy tomato sauce.

Back to Recipes

Ingredients

  • 300g pasta (penne or rigatoni recommended)
  • 2 Vegan Italian Sausages, sliced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup nutritional yeast (for cheesy flavor)
  • Salt, pepper, and fresh basil to taste

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large pan and sauté sliced vegan sausages until browned. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, sauté minced garlic for 1 minute. Add crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth; simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in nutritional yeast, salt, and pepper. Add the cooked pasta and sliced sausage to the sauce.
  5. If the sauce is too thick, stir in reserved pasta water until desired consistency is reached. Serve immediately and garnish with fresh basil.
